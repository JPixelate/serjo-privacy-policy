Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Serjo AI Chatbot (“the Chatbot”, “the Service”) collects, uses, and protects information when users interact with it through Facebook Messenger or other supported platforms.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Serjo AI Chatbot, we may collect the following information:

  • Messages and chat history: Content of conversations between you and the chatbot, stored for the purpose of improving response accuracy and maintaining conversational flow.
  • User-provided data: Such as questions, inquiries, or details you voluntarily share about your requested services.
  • Technical data: Including your Facebook ID (for chat context only) and limited metadata such as timestamps or session identifiers.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial details, passwords, or government-issued IDs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and personalize chatbot responses.
  • To remember conversation context within Messenger.
  • To improve chatbot quality and functionality.
  • To analyze performance and maintain system reliability.

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Chat data is securely stored in our system using standard encryption and access controls. Access to stored data is limited to the chatbot administrator for maintenance and quality improvements.

Your conversation data is retained only as long as needed to provide and improve the Service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third-party organizations or partners, except as required by Facebook’s Platform Policy or applicable law.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The chatbot itself does not use cookies. However, if you access Serjo AI Chatbot through Facebook Messenger or a website, Facebook may collect data under its own Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You can stop using the chatbot at any time by closing or leaving the conversation. Since no personal account is created within our system, individual data deletion or correction requests are generally not applicable.
